鸡公煲 / 煲你喜欢 / 滋滋星
    越菜还是法国菜？Pho Sizzling 滋滋星为您带来源远流长的法国文化与越南美食交织的美味。自成一格的法式越南菜由来自法国的总厨将时尚的烹饪方式与流行的自助理念相结合，帮您把都市的压力与疲惫驱逐一尽，让您在异域的美食中体验一把西式桃花园的魅力。
Vietnamese or French food? Pho Sizzling produces delicious cuisine, drawing on historical confluence of French and Vietnamese cooking. French Chef Benjamin Leib combines traditional cooking style with the popular DIY concept, creating a unique dining experience in which to relax while enjoying the exotic food within a charming environment!
